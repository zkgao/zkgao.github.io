About

I’m an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Western Carolina University. I completed my Ph.D. at NC State University (2025), where I studied how students seek and receive help in programming courses and how AI can responsibly support that process. Before joining WCU, I taught and mentored students across CS1/CS2 and software development courses.

Outside of work, I enjoy cooking and reading mystery novels. I’m passionate about practical learning experiences and building tools that make instructors’ lives easier and students’ lives better.
